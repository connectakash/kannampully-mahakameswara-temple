About the Celebration

Kamolsavam is a unique Thanthric Pooja performed exclusively at KANNAMPULLY MAHAKAMESWARA TEMPLE. During this offering, classical dancers perform directly in front of the Sanctum Sanctorum, presenting their art to the deity itself. Every step, expression, and rhythm becomes an offering, transforming the temple into a sacred stage of devotion.

The performances are completely live and follow prescribed raga, bhava, and costume traditions. Each dancer must complete a continuous 72-minute presentation (3 nazhika), symbolizing unwavering focus and surrender. Kamolsavam begins at dusk, with the offerings commencing at 7:00 PM after the evening pooja, bathed in the glow of temple lamps.

Only women and female transgender artists between the ages of 16 and 56 are permitted to offer this pooja, safeguarding the sanctity and lineage of the tradition. Recognized classical dance forms such as Bharatanatyam, Mohiniyattam, Kuchipudi, Odissi, Kathak, Manipuri, Sattriya, and Nangiar Koothu are welcomed, each adding its own vocabulary of devotion to the festival.
